Dr. Shivakumara Swamiji

The walking God

Here's small glimpse of Dr.Shivakumara Swami's Life

  • 1907 Born in Veerapura,Karnataka
  • 1926 Passed his matricultaion ,he was also residential student at Siddaganga Mutt for a brief span during this time
  • 1930 Joined Siddaganga Mutt,Tumkur
  • 1941 He entered viraktashram .Later became the head of Siddaganga Matha
  • From then onwards he dedicated himself to the development of society .
  • Shivakumara Swamiji founded a total of 132 institutions for education and training,that range from nursery to colleges for engineering,science,arts and management as well as vocational training
  • The swami's Gurukula houses more than 10,000 children from ages five to sixteen years at any point in time and is open to children from all religions, castes, and creeds who are provided free food, education, and shelter (Trividha Daasoha)
  • 2007 He received Karnataka Ratna from the state government
  • 2015 He was honoured with Padma Bhushan ,the third highest civilian award in the republic of India
  • 2018 He lasted his last breath

    • Even though he is no more with us ,his work continuos to benefit lakhs of students and people around the Country.His descendent Shivayogi Swamiji has taken over the charge of Siddaganga Mutta and he is following the footprints of his Guru
